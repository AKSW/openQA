About

Billions of facts pertaining to a multitude of domains are now available on the Web as RDF data. However, accessing this data is still a difficult endeavour for non-expert users. In addition, developing and evaluating question answering systems to alleviate this problem is also a very complex task. We present openQA, a modular open-source framework for the development and evaluation of question answering systems. We show how openQA can be used by integrating two state-of-the-art question answering systems, i.e., SINA and TBSL. The resulting instantiation of openQA can combine the results of the two frameworks as well as use one of the framework as a fallback for the other. We demonstrate the different processing steps from a question to the corresponding answer.
